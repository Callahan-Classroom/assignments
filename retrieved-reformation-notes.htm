Jimmy Valentine is a safecracker.


The story takes place in the early twentieth century.


In the twentieth century, banks were more vulnerable to robbery.


Jimmy was pardoned from prison.


He served ten months of a four-year sentence.


Jimmy only expected to stay three months.


After being released, Jimmy headed straight for a restaurant.


Jimmy had a hotel room in the back of the hotel.


Jimmy had a briefcase full of burglar's tools.


The burglar's tools were made of tempered steel.


A week after Jimmy's release, there was a burglary in Richmond, Indiana.


Someone recognized that Jimmy was the one doing the burglaries.


Jimmy found a woman and forgot that he was a burglar.


The lady went into a bank that Jimmy was about to rob.


Men that looked like Jimmy in the town were rare.
